What can you do with FlexiForms?

Deliver, route and manage forms around real business workflows.

Deliver forms in specific ways

Track individual input with unique per-user links, pre-fill form data from an API or another form, and embed forms into an existing website or software.

Jobs for every use case

Run jobs after submission including emailing, database import, API transfer and exporting form data as a file.

Keep data secure and private

Because FlexiForms can be hosted anywhere and connect to any database or system, you keep control over where data lives and how it is used.

AI enhanced forms

Use AI to create forms faster and interpret resulting data, from questionnaire grading to structured analysis of richer form responses.

How does it work?

Build your form

Easily build complex forms with our AI powered step-by-step wizard. Or if you prefer a hands on approach, use our simple drag and drop interface. Templates help you re-use and distribute form designs easily.

Define your workflow

You can easily integrate a form into an existing business process or application, and define a workflow after a form is submitted by running a series of jobs. For example, generate a PDF and email it, or call an existing API to update a database.

View and manage your forms and data

Our admin interface offers an easy way to configure FlexiForms. Create and edit your form, view your form submissions and even see submission statistics.

Why FlexiForms?

Built for teams that need more than a generic online form.

Typical online form tools

  • Limited question types and standard form patterns
  • Restricted access to your form data
  • Weak support for sensitive data on your own infrastructure
  • Basic delivery options such as a single public link
  • Minimal end-user verification
  • Poor fit for legacy, on-premise or custom software

FlexiForms

  • Dynamic data from APIs and databases
  • Cloud or on-premise hosting options
  • Granular jobs and rules after form input
  • Custom delivery scenarios and embedded experiences
  • AI-assisted form building and data interpretation
  • Direct integration with existing business systems
